## User-Facing Support Text

Need help with MagicBits? Send a short note with your Figma editor type, what you selected, the layout mode you used, and what happened.

MagicBits works offline and does not upload your file content. Please do not send private design files unless you are comfortable sharing them.

## Known Limitations

- Very large selections or very high copy counts can take longer to generate.
- Overlap avoidance is approximate and works best with moderate copy counts.
- Reroll keeps the existing size of items in the selected ScatterBoard.
- Some node types may not support every transform exactly the same way inside Figma and FigJam.
